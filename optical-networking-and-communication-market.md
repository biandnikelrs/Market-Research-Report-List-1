<p><p>Optical networking and communication encompass various market segments essential for efficient data transmission using light signals. Transceivers facilitate bidirectional communication by converting electrical signals to optical signals and vice versa. Amplifiers boost signal strength over long distances. Switches manage data traffic, ensuring optimal routing between networks. Splitters distribute optical signals to multiple outputs, enhancing network reach. Circulators direct light in one direction, improving system performance by minimizing signal loss and interference. Together, these components form the backbone of optical communication systems.</p></p>
