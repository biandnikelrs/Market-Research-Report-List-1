<p><p>Auto dealer accounting software is available in two primary market types: web-based software and installed software. Web-based software operates on cloud platforms, allowing dealers to access their accounting data from any location with internet connectivity, facilitating real-time updates and collaboration. In contrast, installed software is locally hosted on the dealer's hardware, providing greater control over data security and customization but limiting access to specific devices. Both options cater to the varying needs of auto dealerships in managing their financial operations efficiently.</p></p>
