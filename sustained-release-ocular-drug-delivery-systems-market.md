<p><p>Sustained release ocular drug delivery systems encompass various technologies designed to prolong the release of medication to the eye. Liposomes encapsulate drugs in lipid bilayers, enhancing bioavailability and reducing side effects. Microcapsules and nanocapsules offer controlled release by encasing drugs in polymers, suitable for localized delivery. Microspheres and nanospheres, similar to microcapsules, provide sustained release through various matrix configurations. Implants, often placed surgically, deliver drugs over extended periods, directly targeting ocular tissues for improved therapeutic effects.</p></p>
