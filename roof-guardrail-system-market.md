<p><strong>The Roof Guardrail System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-penetrating Guardrail System</li><li>Fixed Guardrail System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The roof guardrail system market comprises two main types: non-penetrating guardrail systems and fixed guardrail systems. Non-penetrating guardrail systems are designed to provide safety without altering the roof structure, making them suitable for sensitive roofing materials. They rely on weight and stability to remain in place. In contrast, fixed guardrail systems involve installing permanent structures that penetrate the roof surface, offering robust protection. Both types are essential for ensuring safety in construction and maintenance activities on rooftops.</p></p>
