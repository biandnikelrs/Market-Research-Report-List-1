<p><p>The Hem Flange (Clinch) Adhesive market is segmented into one-component and two-component types. One-component adhesives are ready-to-use formulations that cure upon application through moisture or heat, offering convenience and reduced preparation time. In contrast, two-component adhesives require mixing before application, providing enhanced bonding strength and versatility for various substrates.
