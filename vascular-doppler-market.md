<p><strong>The Vascular Doppler Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pulse wave vascular doppler</li><li>Continuous wave vascular doppler</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Vascular Doppler market is primarily categorized into two types: pulse wave vascular Doppler and continuous wave vascular Doppler. Pulse wave vascular Doppler operates at high frequencies to provide detailed blood flow information, making it suitable for measuring blood flow in small vessels. In contrast, continuous wave vascular Doppler utilizes lower frequencies, allowing it to measure high-velocity blood flow in larger vessels without depth limitations. Both types are essential for diagnosing vascular conditions and guiding therapeutic interventions.</p></p>
