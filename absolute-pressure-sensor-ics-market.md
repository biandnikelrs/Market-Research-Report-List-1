<p><p>The Absolute Pressure Sensor ICs market is categorized into two main types: Surface Mount and Through Hole. Surface Mount Sensors are designed for compact and high-density applications, offering ease of integration onto printed circuit boards (PCBs) and enabling automated assembly processes. In contrast, Through Hole Sensors feature pins that are inserted into holes on the PCB, providing robust mechanical support and reliability, making them suitable for applications that require enhanced durability. Both types cater to diverse industrial and consumer applications.</p></p>
