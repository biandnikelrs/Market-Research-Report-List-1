<p><strong>The Automotive Wrap Films Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cast Film</li><li>Calendered Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive wrap films are specialized vinyl used for customizing or protecting vehicle surfaces. Cast films, known for their superior clarity and conformability, are ideal for complex curves and intricate designs. They offer long-lasting durability and vibrant colors. In contrast, calendered films are produced through a compression process, making them more economical and less flexible, suitable for flat surfaces and simple applications. Both types serve different market needs, providing options ranging from high-end customization to budget-friendly solutions.</p></p>
