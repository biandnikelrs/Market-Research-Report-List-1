<p><strong>The Speech Recognition Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Speaker Dependent</li><li>Speaker Independent</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The speech recognition market includes two primary types: speaker-dependent and speaker-independent systems. Speaker-dependent systems are tailored to recognize a specific person's voice, requiring user training for accurate results, making them ideal for personalized applications like voice assistants. In contrast, speaker-independent systems are designed to understand speech from any user without prior training, making them suitable for broader applications like virtual assistants or transcription services. Both types serve diverse needs in industries ranging from customer service to healthcare.</p></p>
