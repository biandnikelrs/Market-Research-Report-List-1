<p><strong>The Thermal Infrared Detector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Thermal Infrared Detector</li><li>Passive Thermal Infrared Detector</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thermal infrared detector market consists of two main types: active and passive thermal infrared detectors. Active thermal infrared detectors require an external source of energy to detect thermal radiation, providing enhanced sensitivity and accuracy. In contrast, passive thermal infrared detectors do not require external energy; they detect and convert incoming infrared radiation from objects into electrical signals. Each type serves distinct applications, with active detectors often used in surveillance and imaging, while passive detectors are common in temperature measurement and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
