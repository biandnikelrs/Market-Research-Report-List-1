<p><strong>The Medical Imaging Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spatial Scanning Sensors</li><li>Spectral Scanning Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical imaging sensor market is primarily divided into spatial and spectral scanning sensors. Spatial scanning sensors capture images by scanning the area of interest in a two-dimensional plane, providing detailed structural images, commonly used in modalities like MRI and ultrasound. Conversely, spectral scanning sensors analyze the spectral properties of tissues, enabling the identification of molecular composition, often utilized in techniques like hyperspectral imaging. Both types enhance diagnostic capabilities, enabling better disease detection and treatment monitoring.</p></p>
