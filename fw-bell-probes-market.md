<p><p>FW Bell probes are used for magnetic field measurement and can be categorized into axial and transverse probes. Axial probes measure magnetic fields along the axis of the probe, providing direct readings in applications such as electromagnets and coils. Transverse probes, on the other hand, measure magnetic fields perpendicular to the probe's axis, making them ideal for analyzing fields in complex geometries or evaluating cross-sectional magnetic profiles. Each type is essential for various industrial and research applications.</p></p>
