<p><p>The Anti-Scale Systems market comprises various technologies designed to prevent mineral buildup in water. Softeners use ion exchange to remove hardness-causing minerals like calcium and magnesium, effectively reducing scale formation. Reverse Osmosis (RO) systems utilize a semi-permeable membrane to filter out impurities, including minerals, providing purified water that minimizes scaling. Ion Exchange systems specifically target ions in water, exchanging them for less harmful ones. Together, these technologies enhance water quality, extend equipment lifespan, and improve efficiency in various applications.</p></p>
