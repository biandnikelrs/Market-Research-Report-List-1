<p><p>The GPS receiver market can be broadly classified into differential grade and survey grade categories. Differential grade GPS receivers enhance standard GPS signals with corrections, providing improved accuracy for applications like agriculture and land surveying. Survey grade GPS receivers offer the highest precision, utilizing advanced technology to achieve centimeter-level accuracy, making them ideal for professional surveying, construction, and mapping projects. Both types cater to specific needs, with differential grade being more accessible and survey grade suited for high-stakes accuracy requirements.</p></p>
