<p><p>The commercial waterproofing membranes market encompasses various types, notably modified bitumen and synthetic polymer membranes. Modified bitumen membranes consist of asphalt reinforced with polymers, offering durability and flexibility, making them suitable for diverse climates. Synthetic polymer membranes, such as PVC, TPO, and EPDM, provide exceptional resistance to UV rays, chemicals, and extreme temperatures. Both types are crucial in preventing water infiltration in commercial buildings, enhancing structural integrity and longevity while ensuring energy efficiency and reducing maintenance costs.</p></p>
