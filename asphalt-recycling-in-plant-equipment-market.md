<p><ul><li>Hot Mix Regeneration</li><li>Cold Mix Regeneration</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Asphalt recycling in-plant equipment focuses on two primary market types: hot mix regeneration and cold mix regeneration. Hot mix regeneration involves heating reclaimed asphalt pavement (RAP) to blend with new materials, ensuring high-quality mixes suitable for heavy traffic areas. Cold mix regeneration, on the other hand, utilizes emulsions to combine RAP with minimal heating, making it ideal for low-traffic applications and quick repairs. Both methods enhance sustainability by reducing waste and lowering material costs in road construction and maintenance.</p></p>
