<p><p>The elevator lighting market encompasses various types, primarily recessed lighting and ceiling lighting. Recessed lighting involves fixtures installed into the ceiling, providing a sleek, modern aesthetic while minimizing bulkiness. This type enhances visibility without occupying space, suitable for contemporary designs. Ceiling lighting, on the other hand, includes surface-mounted fixtures that offer direct illumination. It is often used for broader coverage and can accommodate various styles, ensuring safety and accessibility in elevator cabins. Both types contribute to the overall appeal and functionality of the elevator experience.</p></p>
