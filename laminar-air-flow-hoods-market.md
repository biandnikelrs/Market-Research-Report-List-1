<p><p>Laminar air flow hoods are essential devices used in laboratory and medical settings to provide a sterile environment by directing filtered air. The market is segmented into vertical and horizontal flow hoods. Vertical laminar flow hoods have air that flows from the top down, minimizing contamination from personnel and equipment, making them suitable for working with large materials. Horizontal laminar flow hoods direct air horizontally from the back to the front, ideal for smaller items and maximizing workspace accessibility. Both types ensure clean air for sensitive procedures.</p></p>
