<p><p>Indoor turbo trainers are essential for cyclists, offering two main types: classic trainers and smart trainers. Classic trainers provide a basic setup with adjustable resistance, allowing cyclists to maintain fitness indoors. They simulate outdoor riding but lack connectivity. Smart trainers, in contrast, feature advanced technology, including Bluetooth and ANT+ connectivity, enabling integration with cycling apps for real-time data tracking and immersive training experiences.
