<p><strong>The LDPE Granule Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Autoclave Process</li><li>Tubular Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The LDPE granule market is primarily categorized into two production methods: the autoclave process and the tubular process. The autoclave process involves polymerization in a high-pressure, batch-type reactor, yielding low-density polyethylene with a broad molecular weight distribution, suitable for applications requiring flexibility and toughness. In contrast, the tubular process utilizes continuous flow in a vertical reactor, allowing for higher production rates and a more consistent polymer quality, making it ideal for high-volume applications like packaging films and containers.</p></p>
