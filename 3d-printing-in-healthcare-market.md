<p><p>The 3D printing in healthcare market encompasses various technologies, including extrusion, photo-polymerization, and laser beam melting, along with other methods. Extrusion involves depositing materials layer by layer to create structures like prosthetics and implants. Photo-polymerization uses light to cure resins into solid forms, ideal for precise dental applications. Laser beam melting employs high-powered lasers to fuse metal powders, suitable for custom surgical tools and implants. These technologies enhance patient-specific solutions and streamline medical manufacturing processes.</p></p>
