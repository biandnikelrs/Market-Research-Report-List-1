<p><strong>The Road Deflectometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Falling Weight Deflectometer (FWD)</li><li>Light Weight Deflectometer (LWD)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Road Deflectometer market primarily comprises two types: the Falling Weight Deflectometer (FWD) and the Light Weight Deflectometer (LWD). FWD measures pavement stiffness by dropping a known weight from a specific height, assessing structural integrity for road maintenance. In contrast, LWD is a portable device that uses a smaller load to evaluate surface deflection, ideal for quick assessments in construction and rehabilitation projects. Both instruments provide essential data for optimizing road design and maintenance strategies.</p></p>
