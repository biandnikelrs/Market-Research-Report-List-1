<p><strong>The Endotoxin Testing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Turbidimetric</li><li>Chromogenic</li><li>rFC Assays</li><li>Gel Clot</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The endotoxin testing market encompasses various methods used to detect endotoxins, primarily from Gram-negative bacteria. Turbidimetric assays measure the cloudiness in a solution indicating endotoxin presence. Chromogenic assays utilize color change reactions to quantify endotoxins. rFC assays leverage recombinant Factor C from horseshoe crabs for precise detection. Gel clot methods involve a gelation reaction in the presence of endotoxins. Other methods may include molecular techniques and alternative assays, catering to diverse industry needs and regulatory requirements.</p></p>
