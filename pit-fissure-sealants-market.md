<p><p>The Pit Fissure Sealants market comprises primarily two types: resin-based sealants and glass ionomer sealants. Resin-based sealants are known for their durability and ability to bond effectively to tooth surfaces, providing superior protection against decay. They are often preferred for their aesthetic appeal and long-lasting results. In contrast, glass ionomer sealants release fluoride, offering additional protection and are more biocompatible. They are ideal for various clinical situations, particularly in pediatric dentistry where moisture control is challenging.</p></p>
