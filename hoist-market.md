<p><p>The hoist market comprises several types, each designed for specific applications. Manual hoists use hand-operated mechanisms for lifting loads, ideal for lighter tasks. Electric hoists offer powered lifting, suitable for heavier loads and frequent use, enhancing efficiency. Air hoists rely on compressed air, providing a lightweight solution with high lifting capacity, especially in industrial settings. Hydraulic hoists use fluid power for lifting, known for their strength and ability to handle heavy loads safely. Together, these options cater to diverse lifting needs across various industries.</p></p>
