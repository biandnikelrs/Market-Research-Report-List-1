<p><p>The dental ceramic implants market consists of two main types: one-piece and two-piece ceramic implants. One-piece ceramic implants are designed as a single unit, integrating the implant body and abutment, which simplifies installation and reduces potential failure points. In contrast, two-piece ceramic implants feature a separate abutment, allowing for greater customization in angulation and aesthetics. Both types are favored for their biocompatibility, natural appearance, and ability to integrate with bone, catering to patients seeking metal-free dental solutions.</p></p>
