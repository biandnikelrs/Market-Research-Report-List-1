<p><p>CRO services encompass two primary market types: Preclinical CRO and Clinical Trial CRO. Preclinical CROs focus on early-stage research, providing laboratory services to evaluate drug safety and efficacy before human trials. They conduct in vitro and in vivo studies to gather foundational data. Conversely, Clinical Trial CROs manage clinical studies involving human participants, overseeing trial design, patient recruitment, data management, and regulatory compliance. Both types facilitate the drug development process, enhancing efficiency and reducing costs for pharmaceutical companies.</p></p>
