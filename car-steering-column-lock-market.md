<p><strong>The Car Steering Column Lock Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mechanical Steering Column Lock (MSCL)</li><li>Electronic Steering Column Lock (ESCL)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The car steering column lock market comprises two primary types: Mechanical Steering Column Lock (MSCL) and Electronic Steering Column Lock (ESCL). MSCLs utilize a mechanical device, typically a key or lever, to physically secure the steering column, preventing unauthorized movement. ESCLs, on the other hand, are electronically controlled, using sensors and electronic components to immobilize the steering column by disabling the ignition or steering system when needed. Both types enhance vehicle security and deter theft, but they differ in their mechanisms and technologies.</p></p>
